Welcome to power store

The founder Mr. Amodu ore created this store to give weebs the power and ability to feel welcomed and happy this page is dedicated to all things anime as you can see from the home page, the page was designed to make you feel at home.

Mr. Amodu noticed that indeed most online stores don’t have things like cosplay, anime merch and so on and even if they do it most times expensive so what did he do he decided to create a store that will sell quality anime stuffs at an affordable price so that people will have the power to afford it.
